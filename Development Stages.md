
#Status 

#Status/NotStarted**Not Started**

The idea or need is identified, but no concrete planning or resources are allocated.

#Status/Conceptualization**Conceptualization**

Initial brainstorming, defining goals, scope, and high-level requirements.

#Status/PlanningPhase**Planning Phase**

Detailed planning: timelines, resource allocation, technical/semantic/infrastructure design.

#Status/DesignPhase**Design Phase**

Technical architecture, semantic models, and infrastructure blueprints are created.

#Status/ReviewPhase**Review** 

Technical architecture, semantic models, and infrastructure blueprints are being reviewed.

#Status/Prototyping**Prototyping**

Development of a minimal prototype to validate concepts and gather feedback.

#Status/Implementation**Implementation**

Active development: coding, integrating components, and building infrastructure.

#Status/ExtensionPhase  **Extension**

Active extension: coding, integrating components, and building infrastructure.

#Status/Testing**Testing**

Validation of technical, semantic, and infrastructure elements; bug fixes and optimizations.

#Status/Deployment**Deployment**

Rollout of the framework in a live or pilot environment.

#Status/Monitoring**Monitoring & Maintenance**

Ongoing support, updates, and scaling based on feedback and performance.

#Status/Deprecation**Deprecation**

Phase-out or replacement of outdated components or the entire framework.
